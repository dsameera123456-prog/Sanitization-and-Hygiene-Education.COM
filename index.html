About CSP

Engineering learning connected with public health action

Community Service Project (CSP) is an experiential learning program that connects engineering students with society to identify real-world problems and provide awareness-based solutions. This project focuses on sanitation, hygiene, environmental cleanliness, and public health awareness in Gooty.

Promote personal hygiene
Improve environmental cleanliness
Create disease prevention awareness
Encourage community participation
Educate children and youth
Promote safe drinking water practices
